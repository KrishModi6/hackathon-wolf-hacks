Features & Vision

BramHealth addresses Brampton 2040 Vision 6 through five strategic pillars, grounded in STEAM principles (Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts, Mathematics).

Our STEAM Foundation

Science

Symptom logic, mental health support, health tracking

Technology

Website, AI chat, symptom checker

Engineering

System flow, care pathways, modular design

Arts

Clean UI, accessible design, calming interfaces

Math

Scoring, risk levels, tracking, rewards

Key Capabilities Implemented

🎤 Voice-First Symptoms

No typing required—describe symptoms with efficiency. Perfect for elderly, mobility-limited patients.

🧠 Mental Health Hub

Age-aware AI, calm room environment, supervised youth forum, anonymous ethical support chats.

🏃 Active Living Rewards

Trackable ML-based fitness system. Exercise → Brampton city credits. Preventative health incentivized.

📊 Outbreak & Surge Modes

Real-time condition_x detection. Heatwave and cyberattack resilience. Automated routing under stress.

🔐 HIPAA & Encrypted Data

AWS-backed secure sharing. Bias-aware ethical decision-making. Immunocompromised flagging.

🚑 Mobile Health Integration

CAD-designed facilities, modular care units, live wait-time optimization, family-mode triage.
